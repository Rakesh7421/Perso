Upload Guidelines
Requirements
  • File size must be under 16MB
  • CSV files must have headers
  • Scheduled times must be in the future
  • Post content must be under 3000 characters
  • Use UTF-8 encoding for special characters
Tips
  • Test with a small file first
  • Include timezone in your timestamps
  • Use quotes around content with commas (CSV)
  • Check for duplicate scheduled times
  • Validate data before uploading
